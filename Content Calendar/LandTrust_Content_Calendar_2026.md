## Content Optimization Checklist (Every Post)

- [ ] **Internal links:** 2+ posts in same pillar, 1+ cross-pillar, 1+ to LandTrust marketplace page
- [ ] **FAQ schema:** Structured Q&A section with 3-5 questions
- [ ] **Direct answers:** First 100 words of each section directly answer the implied question
- [ ] **State specificity:** References specific states/regions (AI engines cite location-specific content)
- [ ] **CTA:** Relevant call-to-action (browse properties, list your land, sign up for LandTrust+)
- [ ] **Meta:** Title tag < 60 chars, meta description < 155 chars with target keyword
- [ ] **Headers:** H2s use target keywords, H3s use related long-tail variants
