## The Opportunity

LandTrust's current AI Visibility Score is **30 out of 100**. Monthly audience sits at 3.71M but is **declining by 92,000+ visitors per month**. That trend needs to reverse.

The good news: Semrush identified **414 untapped topic opportunities** where LandTrust has zero presence despite being directly relevant to the business. These topics carry **combined AI search volume in the millions** -- people actively searching for hunting seasons, licenses, gear recommendations, species guides, and landowner resources that LandTrust should own.

On top of that, LandTrust is currently **missing from 50+ high-traffic AI prompts** -- queries like "best hunting rifles," "hunter safety course," "best choke for duck hunting," and "how to cook dove breast" where competitors are getting cited and LandTrust isn't.

This strategy closes those gaps with **68 targeted blog posts** over 34 weeks, prioritized by search volume and business impact.

## Why This Strategy Works

**1. It's data-driven, not guesswork.** Every post maps to a specific Semrush topic with measurable search volume or a documented missed AI prompt. We're not creating content and hoping it ranks -- we're filling verified gaps where demand already exists.

**2. It's prioritized by impact.** The first posts published capture the largest opportunities (1.03M licenses topic, 250K wild boar topic, 91K Virginia seasons). By the end of Phase 1, LandTrust will have content competing for over 1.5M in monthly AI search volume that it currently captures zero of.

**3. It feeds the marketplace.** State season guides funnel directly to LandTrust state property pages. Landowner content recruits new property listings. Gear and species content builds trust with hunters who become bookers. Every post has a commercial purpose beyond SEO.

**4. It builds compounding authority.** Search engines and AI systems reward topical depth. As LandTrust publishes multiple posts within each pillar, its authority on those topics grows -- making every subsequent post in that cluster rank faster and get cited more often.

**5. It's seasonally timed.** Turkey content publishes before spring turkey season. State guides go live during summer planning season. Waterfowl and deer content drops before fall. Dove recipes publish during dove season. The content meets hunters when they're actively searching.

**6. It serves both sides of the marketplace.** 8 posts specifically target landowners with content about revenue, liability, guest management, and succession planning. This isn't just a hunter acquisition strategy -- it's a supply growth strategy.

## Content Standards

Every post will include:

- **FAQ sections** with structured data markup for AI engine visibility
- **Direct answers** in the first 100 words of each section (this is what AI engines extract and cite)
- **State-specific references** throughout (AI engines heavily favor location-specific content)
- **Internal links** to related LandTrust content and marketplace pages
- **Clear calls to action** (browse properties, list your land, start a free trial)
