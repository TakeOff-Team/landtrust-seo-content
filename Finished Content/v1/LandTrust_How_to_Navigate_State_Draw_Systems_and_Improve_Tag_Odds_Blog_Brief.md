### 2) How State Draw Systems Work (At a Glance)
- Definitions with plain‑English diagrams:
  - Preference points (e.g., CO general, WY NR deer/elk): highest points get priority on a share of tags.
  - Bonus points (e.g., AZ, UT LE): points are extra “tickets,” still random but weighted.
  - Pure lottery/random (e.g., NM) with some resident/NR allocations.
  - Hybrids and modified splits (e.g., split pools between points and random).
- Where each model shines/fails from a hunter perspective (speed to tag vs trophy targeting).

### 3) Key Levers to Improve Your Odds (What Actually Moves the Needle)
- Choice ordering and realistic first choices; using 2nd–4th choices strategically.
- Party applications: when to group, how averages/lowest‑point rules work by state, pros/cons.
- Unit filtering: target opportunity units, weapon/season pivots (archery/muzzle vs rifle), and lower‑pressure dates.
- Point creep management: targeting mid‑tier units, alternating species, using youth/mentor pathways where applicable.
- Returned/reissued/leftover strategies (state by state nuances), and alerts.
- OTC vs Draw portfolio: hunt annually for experience (OTC/general) while banking points for LE/trophy.

### 4) State‑by‑State Quick Guide (2025)
- Visual/table callout: application windows, point type, leftover/reissue notes, and a "quick odds lever."
- Example entries to include (expand in body text):
  - Colorado (CPW): preference points for many limited hunts; large OTC opportunity; primary draw in March; reissue/leftover list; watch 2028 system updates.
  - Arizona (AZGFD): bonus points, 20% pass to highest points on some hunts; strategy: mix choices, consider late hunts.
  - Utah (UT DWR): bonus for LE and once‑in‑a‑lifetime; preference for general deer; consider spike/general elk for annual reps.
  - New Mexico (NMDGF): pure lottery, no points; emphasis on choice strategy and group apps.
  - Wyoming (WGFD): preference for NR elk/deer/antelope; special vs regular draws; regional strategies.
  - Montana (FWP): preference for NR combos; bonus for LE permits; general elk/deer pathways.
