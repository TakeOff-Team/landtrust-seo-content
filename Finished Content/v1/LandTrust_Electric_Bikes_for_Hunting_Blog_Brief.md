### **2. What Makes an E‑Bike “Hunting‑Ready” (Beyond Fat Tires)**
- Motor & drivetrain
  - Mid‑drive vs hub: torque at low cadence on hills; weight distribution; serviceability.
  - AWD options (pros/cons): traction vs complexity and battery draw.
- Power & torque
  - 500W vs 750W vs 1000W: where extra torque matters (steep grades, towing).
  - Controller current limits and “real” feel vs labeled wattage.
- Payload & braking
  - Calculate system weight: rider + pack + trailer + game; what components limit payload.
  - Brake types (4‑piston hydraulics), rotor size, and fade on descents.
- Rolling chassis
  - Tires: width, tread, sidewalls for thorns/rocks; insert options.
  - Suspension choices: coil vs air, hardtail vs full‑suspension tradeoffs.

### **3. Range Where It Matters: Hills, Trailers, and Cold Weather**
- Range drivers
  - Gradient and surface (double‑track vs loose timber); rider cadence and PAS level.
  - Load effects: towing 50 lb vs 150 lb; stop‑and‑go in brush.
  - Temperature: lithium performance at 30–40°F vs 70°F.
- Mitigation
  - Battery strategy: carry a spare; insulate; keep warm until deploy; charging cadence in camp or truck.
  - Gearing & cadence to reduce current spikes; eco vs trail PAS.
- Quick‑reference (writer to build a small table)
  - Flat/no load vs +50 lb trailer vs +150 lb trailer; 70°F vs 30°F expected % range change.

### **4. Towing Game Safely with Trailers**
- Trailer options
  - Single‑wheel vs dual‑wheel stability; width in brush; rollover risk.
  - Hitches: axle vs seatpost; compatibility checks.
- Secure & haul
  - Straps, netting, and balance; low center of gravity; avoid heel strike.
  - Descents: braking technique, rear‑bias weight, walking steep pitches.
- Field workflow
  - Quartering vs whole: when to split; multiple trips vs one heavy pull.
  - Leave no trace on private land; gate/road etiquette with owners.

### **5. Quiet, Stealth, and Scent Management**
- Noise reality
  - Motor/drive whine at PAS vs throttle; chain/tire noise on gravel.
  - Ride posture and speed to keep acoustic profile low.
- Scent & approach
  - Work with wind; stage bike before final approach.
  - Minimize stops near bedding/feeding; avoid brake squeal.

### **6. Legal Access: Public vs Private**
- Classes & policy
  - Class 1/2/3 definitions; throttle vs pedal assist implications.
  - BLM/USFS: many non‑motorized trails exclude e‑bikes; cite to official pages.
- Private‑land advantages
  - Owner permission, clear rules, lower user conflict, insurance and guest verification through LandTrust.
  - How to confirm e‑bike allowances on individual listings; messaging owners.
- CTA: Browse properties where e‑bikes are allowed on‑property roads or access routes.

- Stability & safety

- Transport

### **9. Pre‑Hunt Checklist & Plan**
- Gear & bike
  - Battery/batteries charged; insulated storage; charger packed if applicable.
  - Tire pressure set for surface; brake check; lights; multitool and plugs.
  - Trailer pins/clips/straps; spare strap; gloves; headlamp.
- Route & rules
  - Map approach; range estimate with margin; bailout plan.
  - Confirm property rules (roads, gates, quiet hours); mark parking/turnaround.
- Owner comms
  - Message ETA, plan, and any special access needs; confirm gates/locks.
  - CTA: View and follow the listing on LandTrust for any owner updates.
