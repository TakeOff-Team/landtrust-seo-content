# LandTrust Business Overview

## Company Name
**LandTrust** (The Recreation Access Network)

## Tagline or Slogan
"Find your next trip on private land" / "The best way to hunt private land"

## About / Company Description
LandTrust is the leading recreation access network that connects landowners with outdoor enthusiasts seeking private land access. Founded in 2019, they operate an online marketplace that brings together farmers, ranchers, and landowners with hunters, fishermen, and outdoor recreators. The company believes the future of working lands depends on a portfolio of income from agricultural production, conservation, and recreation opportunities. They're committed to building partnership, community, and trust while enabling the enjoyment, profitability, and sustainability of America's undeveloped private lands.

## Primary Product(s) or Service(s)
- **Online Marketplace Platform**: Connects landowners with outdoor recreators
- **LandTrust+ Premium Membership**: $299/year subscription offering early access to new properties, draw protection, field notes updates, and VIP brand discounts
- **Safety & Insurance Program**: Includes guest ID verification, credit card prepayment, liability waivers, participant insurance, and $1M general liability policy
- **Field Notes**: Real-time updates from landowners about game cam photos, new packages, and property conditions

## Key Business Model
LandTrust operates as a two-sided marketplace platform, earning revenue through:
1. **Commission-based model** - taking a percentage of successful bookings
2. **Premium subscriptions** - LandTrust+ membership at $299/year
3. **Partnership revenue** - brand partnerships and gear discounts
